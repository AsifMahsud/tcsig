Public School Works

As a member of the TCSIG workers’ compensation program, members will receive services from PublicSchoolWORKS (PSW) which are being funded by TCSIG. PSW is a comprehensive online workplace safety program that includes programs and services that have been developed to improve the safety of schools and focuses on prevention initiatives to reduce risk but also post incident initiatives to mitigate risk issues when an employee injury occurs.

Safety National De-escalation Resources

Safety National has two de-escalation vendors they have partnered with: Crisis Prevention Institute (CPI) and ProAct. They both focus on de-escalation but have different philosophies of the approach and audience. Discount pricing is available.


CPI - https://www.crisisprevention.com/Industries/Education-Professionals
  • Focuses on verbal de-escalation but also addresses physical techniques to reduce injuries in the event of physical contact.
  • Broader target audience extends from staff & teachers up to security and School Resource Officers (SROs).
  • Safety National has secured discount pricing for the following packages & courses:
    • Verbal Intervention Training
    • Nonviolent Crisis Intervention Training
    • Nonviolent Crisis Intervention with Advanced Physical Skills Training
    • Nonviolent Crisis Intervention Specialized Renewal – Autism Spectrum
    • Nonviolent Crisis Intervention Specialized Renewal – Trauma
ProACT - https://proacttraining.com/
  • Has more proactive, preventative approach to the assault cycle. It focuses on prevention and de-escalation during the verbal exchanges which often precede violence. It is a hands-off approach.
  • Geared more toward teachers and staff.
  • Safety National closing in on contract with discount pricing for:
    • Pro-ACT Training
    • Pro-ACT Advantage e-Learning
