About Us/Mission

The Tri-County Schools Insurance Group, Joint Powers Authority (JPA), was formed in 1983 for the purpose of establishing and maintaining property, liability, health, dental, vision and group life. In 2019, TCSIG JPA now includes a workers' compensation program.
