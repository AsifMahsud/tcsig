Happy New Year from Ryan Robison, JPA President

Happy New Year! I wish you all a safe, happy and healthy 2018. It has been an honor to serve you as President of our JPA’s Executive Board this past year. I have enjoyed working together with the Executive Board, TCSIG CEO, Matt Evans, TCSIG office staff, our service providers and, most importantly, you, our TCSIG members and your families. Together we form one of the most successful JPA’s in California. As exciting and productive as 2017 has been for our organization, 2018 holds even more promise. As we move forward into the New Year, it is important to remember that the key to our success continues to rest on our rock solid foundation established in our humble beginnings over 40 years ago. “All For One and One For ALL” rings true today more than ever.
