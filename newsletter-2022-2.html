Welcome TCSIG members and employees!

In this issue, we’ll be reviewing the new Pharmacy Benefits Manager, Ingenio Rx, Annual Biometric Screening, and the 2022-23 TCSIG rate settings. We’re also providing updates on some of the Health and Wellness services available to you as a TCSIG member employee.

Annual Biometric Screenings have begun!

Complete your screening onsite or at the TCSIG Wellness Center. To schedule your appointment, call the Wellness Center at (530) 822-5500 or schedule online through Healow.
Eligible participants will receive a $50 gift card and can review results with one of the top-rated clinicians at the Wellness Center. Your clinician will work with you to analyze your Biometric Screening so you can better understand your health today and build plans for a healthier tomorrow.
For more information contact the Wellness Center or speak with your HR representative to find out when the Wellness Center will be visiting you.
