NEW PHARMACY BENEFITS MANAGER: INGENIO RX

Dear TCSIG Members,

It is time to get ready for your new pharmacy benefits manager

Pharmacy is an important part of your health benefits. Starting July 1, 2022, Ingenio Rx will manage your prescription drug coverage. Please watch the mail at the end of June for your new ID card. You will need to present this card when you use your pharmacy benefits.

What this means for you

If you are currently getting a prescription from a retail pharmacy, you may be able to continue receiving your refills from that pharmacy using your new ID card.

We are here to help

If you have questions about this change or your plan, please call (530) 822-5299.

Sincerely,
TCSIG Admin

2022-2023 RATES ANNOUNCED

On March 25, 2022, the JPA Board approved the FY 2022/2023 rates and there are NO RATE CHANGES for the 6th year in a row on PPO Plans. The summary is listed below:
Medical PPO Plans: 0% RATE INCREASE
Dental Plans: 0% RATE INCREASE
Vision Plans: 0% RATE INCREASE
Waiver Assessment: 0% RATE INCREASE
Group Life Plans: 0% RATE INCREASE
Voluntary Life Plans: 0% RATE INCREASE
Kaiser Medical Plans: Approximately 6% increase, with Senior Advantage Plans (Medicare) decreasing.​

January 2022 Prescription Changes

Some members received letters from ProAct indicating changes required in their prescriptions however TCSIG worked with ProAct to grandfather prescriptions that were affected by the changes. Members may either switch to the suggested prescription or continue to fill their current prescription. If a member is still experiencing problems getting a prescription please contact ProAct at (877) 635-9545 or TCSIG at (530) 822-5299.

2020-2021 RATES ANNOUNCED

Great News!
On April 24, 2020, the JPA Board approved the FY 2020/2021 rates and there are NO RATE CHANGES for the 4th year in a row on PPO Plans and all medical plans have been enhanced. The summary is listed below:
